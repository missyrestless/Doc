To Install pack on the Intan Couple Dance Ball

Rez the "ICDB Animation Installer" version of the pack close to your Intan Couple Dance Ball, the installer will ask permission to animate, if this does not happen you may need to rez the installer closer to the dance ball.

Once you accept the permission request the installer will ask you to say the word "install" in local chat to begin the installation, the animations will be installed and the Intan Couple Dance Ball will reset and add them to the menu system.



To install pack on the TIS couples/hybrid and older non v3 Intan Couple Dance Balls::

Rez the TiS/Intan ready pack, left click and "copy to inventory", this will make a folder of the same name in your inventory containing the animations.

Right click and edit the TiS couples/Hybrid or Intan couple dance ball and press the contents tab, drag the folder mentioned above into those contents, this will copy the dance animations into the machine.

Current versions of the TiS couples/Hybrid and Intan couple dance ball automatically configure the installed dance and after a few seconds it will be available in the machines menu.
