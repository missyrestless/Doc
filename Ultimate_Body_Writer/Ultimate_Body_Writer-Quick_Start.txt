QuickStart Guide for the Venus's Closet - Ultimate Body Writer!


This guide will give you quick overview of how to use this Body Writer.


____________________________WRITE / ERASE __________________________

1) To write, you need to select the [Write] button. This will open the available areas for writing. 
2) Choosing an area will give you a list of the available spots in that area. Spots marked with \* will also give you the option to add an arrow towards either the Ass or the Pussy Penis (depending if the area is from behind or front). You can un-tick the Arrow option and press [Write] to open the text box to write. Arrow can be selected randomly from a list of arrows if you tick the option [☐-Random].
Choosing an area without arrow, opens the text box to write immediately.

3) Write you message - up to 16 characters and press [Submit] to write.

Choosing Erase will give you the same list of areas and writing points to erase what is written there.


___________________Temporary Color / Temporary Font ________________
Depending on the Wearer’s choices there might be a [Color (temp)] and/or a [Font (temp)] button. Those two will allow you to choose a temporary Color and/or Font respectively. The selection remains active only while you are using the menu.
