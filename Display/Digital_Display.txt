Digital Display v2.1
░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░

Neat and modern looking all-in-one gadget, displays your advertising, invite users to your group, hands out landmark, gifts, notecards and up to 5 links to your social media, blog or websites! 
From a quiet gadget that just displays a slide show of your ads, to a more interactive tool, you are in control, one settings notecard, with instructions to setup each option.

Main features:

      ✔ Slide show or static presentation of your advertising or pictures, works with 4x3. 1x1, 1x2 aspect ratio textures.
      ✔ Slide show automatic for 2 or more pictures.
      ✔ Slide show with optional fade effect.
      ✔ Dialog on click with optional buttons for Owner IM, Owner Profile, Join Group, Gifts, Landmark, Notecard, up to 5 links to your social media pages, website or blog.
      ✔ All options on a click dialog menu which can be enabled or disable. 
      ✔ Option to notify you when someone clicks on the board..
      ✔ One settings notecard, with instructions for each option, you are in control!
      ✔ Modern look, flat design with rounded display panels, enhances your product/ad presentation!
      ✔ Single sided and Double sided models included.
      ✔ 2 LI (Single and Double Panels).
      ✔ 6 models included (4x3, 1x1, 1x2 in single and double sided panels)
      ✔ 10 PBR materials on menu plus 1 fallback.

All settings are set per board, which means, you can assign different settings for each location... from quiet to interactive, specific information for each, etc.
You may setup one board, and take a copy, using it as a template for all your locations. (Rez it wherever you like, change its Location Name... that's it!)

Setup
░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░

1-) Add one or more pictures/textures (logo or related texture) to the gadget contents (Edit, Content tab). If more than one texture is found, the gadget will display a slide show (fade effect) showing the pictures in the same sequence they are found.

2-) A notecard named "Settings" is available inside the gadget (Edit, Content tab), please open it (double click) and edit as needed, instructions for each option are included in the same notecard.

3-) Click the gadget and select "On" on menu. (Make sure to turn Off and On again after making changes to the Settings notecard.)

░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░
Menu
░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░

Options:

[On] - Turns the gadget on reading current settings.

[Off] - Turns the gadget off.

[Test Click] - Allows you to see the dialog sent when an avatar clicks on the gadget. You will see all buttons according to your settings.

[Manual] - Hands out this manual, if still available inside the gadget.

[Done] - Close the menu.
