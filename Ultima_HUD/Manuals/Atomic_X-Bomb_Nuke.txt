[SET UP]

IMPORTANT : Be sure to be in a rez and script allowed area and wear the latest version of Ultima HUD

Rez the object "*Ultima HUD* Add-On X-Bomb" included in this package and TOUCH IT to transfer its contents to your Ultima HUD.
Once the new features added to your HUD, touch again the device to delete it. Keep the copy of your device in you inventory.

Now you can access to the new features added by clicking the HUD + button (just under its logo), this add-on will be listed in "Attacks" chapter.
__________________________________________________________
[FEATURES DETAILS & USAGE]

USAGE :
▶ Rez the X-Bomb ◀ 
Click the + button of your Ultima HUD and select the "Attacks" chapter, then click on "X-Bomb".

▶ Set-up a countdown ◀ 
Touch the X-Bomb metallic case to open it. A dialog box will show up with different predefined countdowns (from 10 sec. to 60 min.)
Select the countdown time of your choice. You can also define a custom countdown by selecting "*Custom*" and then enter the desired value in seconds in the next text-box, and submit.
Once selected, the countdown will start immediately and display on screen.

▶ Stop ◀ 
To stop the countdown, simply click the screen and select "STOP" in the dialog box.

▶ Abort / Delete ◀ 
Select "!DELETE!" to erase the X-Bomb nuke case.

OPTIONS :
▶ Restart the countdown (after stopping) ◀ 
Touch the screen, and select "Restart" in the dialog box to restart a previously stopped countdown, you can also select a new countdown time.

▶ Open / Close the metallic case ◀
Open / Close the metallic case by simply touching it, it won't affect the countdown which will continue if already started, 

EXPLOSION :
At the end of the countdown, the X-Bomb will explode, creating a huge fireball with push effects in a wide range (>= 100m.) affecting all surrounding avatars and physical objects (including you).

CRATER :
The explosion will leave behind a giant crater which will self delete after 120 seconds (2 minutes)
