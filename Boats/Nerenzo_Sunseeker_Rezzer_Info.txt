Hi,
this is an overview how to use the rezzer:
=======================================

A) ADJUST

to ADJUST the position where the boat rezzes please:

1) click on the sign/rock and select "adjust pos"
2) a red boat will rez - position this boat wherever you want within a range of 10 meters 
3) if you closed the menu from the rezzer click the sign again, select "get data"
4) copy the 3 lines from the local chat - click "kill boat"
5) click with your RIGHT mousebutton on the rezzer and go to the tab content
6) open the NC called "config"
7) scroll down to the end of the NC, delete the old rotation/position and paste your new one
8) save the NC
9) IMPORTANT -> click the sign/rock and select "read NC"
=======================================

B) PERMISSION

to change the PERMISSION who can rezz the boat:

1) click with your right mousebutton @ the sign/rock
2) go to the tab "content"
3) scroll down to the line "access_type="
4) change from public to either "owner" or "group"
5) save the NC
6) IMPORTANT -> click the sign and select "read NC"

special settings: you can also allow specific avatars to rezz the boat:
1) change the permission to "owner"
2) add the line "add_avatar=" under the line permission
3) copy past the avatar UUID; you can find in the profile of your friend after the "="
4) example, if you want me to be added, the line would be "add_avatar=8237b75c-b3b0-4fe1-9a3f-2e14eb5ab339"
5) the count of this command is "almost without a limit". BUT: set ANOTHER LINE for every avatar
=======================================

C) DENIED MESSAGE

to set a new "DENIED MESSAGE" 

1) click with your right mousebutton @ the sign/rezzer
2) go to the tab "content"
3) scroll down to the line "denied_message="
4) change the text
5) save the NC
6) IMPORTANT -> click the sign and select "read NC"
=======================================
PLEASE NOTE:
-) every person other than the owner does not get a menu from the rezzer, the boat will be rezzed by touch.
-) each boat will derezz (simwide) when the boat is empty for more than 30 seconds
