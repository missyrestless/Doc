=======================================================
        EASY STREAMING TV - USER GUIDE
=======================================================

Welcome to the Easy Streaming TV! This powerful and easy-
to-use system allows you to watch videos, playlists, and
live streams in Second Life with just a few clicks.

You can watch alone or with others, use playlists, or
stream live content for synchronized viewing.

=======================================================
QUICK START
=======================================================

1. Rez the TV on your land
2. Click anywhere on the TV frame to turn it on
3. The menu will appear

4. Choose one of the following:
   • Enter a URL
   • Playlist
   • Streaming TV
   • YouTube Web

5. Click the screen to load the video
6. Use Play to begin playback
7. Enjoy!

=======================================================
SUPPORTED SERVICES
=======================================================

The Easy Streaming TV supports:

• YouTube
• Vimeo
• Twitch
• DailyMotion
• Pluto TV
• Tubi TV
• Kosmi TV Sharing
• ShoutCast Radio
• Kick

=======================================================
VIEWING METHODS
=======================================================

ENTER URL
Play a video directly from a link:
• Click "YouTube URL"
• Paste your video link
• Click Submit

PLAYLIST
Use a notecard inside the TV:
• Add a playlist notecard to the TV
• Click Playlist → Change List
• Select a video or Play All

YOUTUBE WEB
Browse YouTube directly:
• Click "YouTube Web"
• Search and play videos

NOTE:
YouTube Web mode does NOT support synchronized viewing

=======================================================
PLAYLIST FORMAT
=======================================================

Each line must follow this format:

Button Name|Video Title|YouTubeURL

RULES:

• Button name must be 13 characters or less
• One video per line
• No special characters or curly quotes
• No duplicate videos
• No extra spaces or symbols

Each playlist notecard supports up to 50 videos

=======================================================
PLAYBACK CONTROLS
=======================================================

PLAY
Starts the current video or playlist

STOP
Stops playback

NOTE:
Auto Play was removed in Rev 5.0

=======================================================
SYNCHRONIZED VIEWING
=======================================================

To watch together:

1. Load your video
2. Click Stop
3. Ask everyone to click the screen
4. Click Play

All viewers will start at the same time

NOTE:
If someone leaves or pauses, they will fall out of sync

=======================================================
LIVE STREAMING
=======================================================

True synchronization only works with live streams

Examples:

• YouTube Live
• RTMP / HLS streams

TIP:
If you stream using OBS, send it to YouTube Live and
use the live URL on the TV

=======================================================
SOUND SETTINGS
=======================================================

To hear audio across the parcel:

Preferences → Sound & Media

Set:

• Starts getting quieter = 512
• Completely disappears = 512

=======================================================
PRIM MEDIA SOUND BLEED
=======================================================

Prim Media may be heard across nearby parcels because
it is not restricted by parcel boundaries in all cases.

FIX OPTIONS:

• Use the Land Media Connector for parcel-based playback

• Use Play/Stop responsibly when others are nearby

• Firestorm Viewer Setting:

  Go to About Land → Sound tab

  Check:
  "Restrict MOAP to this parcel"

This setting helps prevent media from your TV from
being heard outside your parcel.

=======================================================
COMMON ISSUES
=======================================================

TV NOT STARTING:
• Click the screen
• Make sure Media is enabled
• Be on the same parcel
• Try Reload

MEDIA NOT WORKING:
• Clear viewer cache
• Restart viewer
• Test on a simple prim

WHITE SCREEN:
• Clear history
• Reload

GREEN SCREEN:
• Rez a new TV
• Reduce script load

=======================================================
DRM ISSUES
=======================================================

Some services may not work:

• Netflix
• Hulu
• Peacock
• Some Pluto/Tubi content

Cause:
DRM restrictions and viewer limitations

=======================================================
"NOT A BOT" MESSAGE
=======================================================

Cause:
Too many rapid requests to YouTube

Fix:
• Wait
• Restart router

Reduce chance:
• Avoid rapid clicking
• Avoid repeated reloads
• Use playlists

=======================================================
VIDEO NOT AVAILABLE
=======================================================

Causes:

• Region restrictions
• Age restrictions
• Playback disabled by owner

Playlist note:
One bad video breaks the playlist

Fix:
Remove restricted video

=======================================================
SCREEN GOES BLANK
=======================================================

Cause:
Viewer stops rendering media when not visible

Fix:
• Stay near screen
• Increase draw distance
• Add additional screens
