Lynx by Eredita: Keep Your Connections Alive

Ever met someone in Second Life who made an impression, but you're not ready to add them to your friends list? Maybe you had a great chat with a fellow creator or someone special caught your eye at an event. Don't let those connections fade away—Lynx helps you keep track without any commitment.

Stay Connected Without the Clutter

Lynx is a sleek, simple menu-driven HUD that lets you store and monitor the online status of anyone in Second Life—even if they're not on your friends list. It's your personal social radar.

ADDING A FRIEND

Step 1: Wear the Lynx HUD

Step 2: Click the Lynx HUD button

Step 3: In the popup menu, click Add

Step 4: In the Text box, enter either your friend's Account Name or UUID Key. See below for more information.

ACCOUNT NAME FORMATS

For accounts with First and Last names:
FirstName LastName
FirstName.Lastname

For accounts with only a Username:
Username Resident
Username.Resident
Username

*Note: Online status updates may experience delays of up to 10 minutes due to Second Life server limitations.

EXPORTING FRIENDS LIST - LYNX PLUS ONLY

Step 1: Click the Lynx HUD button

Step 2: In the popup menu, click Export

This will cause your entire stored friends list to print to your chat via the Owner channel, so only you will be able to see it. Each friend will have their full Account Name and UUID Key displayed, allowing you to copy everything for your own safekeeping.
