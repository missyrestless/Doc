                            FITTING THE PRODUCT
_____________________________________________________________


The Ultimate Foot Clicker is designed to work with all bodies and footwear. Simply right click and edit the product to make it fit your shape. Its single prim nature allows you to stretch it with precision. Make sure that the clicker is slightly larger than your foot/shoe so that when people attempt to click it they don't accidentally click your foot/shoe instead.
