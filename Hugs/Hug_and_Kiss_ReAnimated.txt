# Quick start 
#

* On purchase you will receive a product folder called "* S&H * Hug & Kiss 3 - ReAnimated". It contains this note card, a landmark to Strike & Heston's Animations and an object called "* S&H * Hug & Kiss 3.0 - ReAnimated". Right click it in your inventory and choose "add" or "wear". You will now see the HUD appear on the top right side of the screen. 
* Face a person you would like to hug or kiss
* Click the center button. A blue menu will pop up with names of people who are close enough to invite, choose your victim from the list to send an invitation. When the other accepts, hugs will happen!

################
#
# Updating
#

Updates are automatically provided through the Casper Update system. each time the object is rezzed, it will check wether your version is still up to date. When then is a new version, you will get an update dialog where you can choose whether to receive it or not.

YES! - receive the update
Not now - You will be offered the dialog again the next time the HUD is rezzed, after one hour is past. 
Postpone - Decline this update, wait for the next version
Never - Never receive updates again

If you accidentally picked the wrong option, you can always get the update manually by having it redelivered through the vendor or marketplace.

# Hints, Tips and frequently asked questions
#


* The height matching system happens automatically without any need for you to set any preferences except for Male or Female.

* When you attach the Hug & Kiss to a new attachment point on your screen, it will automatically be placed in an appropriate position. You can adjust this to fit, and will remember the adjustments when you reattach to the same point.

* Other attachments may cause the Hug & Kiss to fail, keeping the two participants to far away from each other to make the animations fit. This are often anti push scripts or shields.
