# Firestorm Tips

A few tips for users of the Firestorm Viewer.

## Search your inventory with a wildcard using the plus sign

    larax+dress
    purple+latex+socks

## Keyboard shortcuts

- `ctrl+alt+p` - Toggle movelock so you don't get run over
- `alt + shift + h`  - Toggle showing HUD displays 
- `alt + shift + u`  -  Toggle all menus items and buttons
- `ctrl + shift + alt + 2`  - May reduce lag in very crowded sims (toggles non-avatar alphas)  
- In local chat type:
  - `calc` – use the viewer as a calculator, type calc then your formula then enter (eg: `calc 2+2`)
  - `dd` – draw distance, type in dd then number (eg: `dd 64` brings your draw distance to 64 meters)
  - `flr` to teleport to ground level
  - `gtp` – go to position, type in gtp then the coordinates you want to move to (eg: `gtp 10 10 10`)
  - `mapto region name` – tp to a specific region (eg: `mapto Lime` teleports you to the Lime Sim)
  - `rolld` – rolls dice, use like this: `rolld <# dice> <# faces>` (eg: `rolld 2 20` would be 2 dice with 20 faces each) more info HERE. https://wiki.firestormviewer.org/fs_dice
  - `tp2cam` – teleports you to where your camera is looking
  - `tp2 Carrie` (teleport on top of your friend's head. I swear.)
  - `tph` – telelport home, teleports you to where you have your home set to
- While in the air
  - type `rezplat` in local to rez a platform prim to stand on
  - type `gth 2000` to teleport to 2000m
 
## View Animation Details

To view the details of an animation, including its duration:

- Locate the animation in your inventory
- Right click the animation and select `Open`
- Click the down arrow to the right of the `Play Locally` button
