Key Camera Shortcuts (Windows/Linux/Mac)
--------------------

Zoom In/Out: Alt + Mouse Wheel Scroll, or Alt + Up/Down Arrow.

Rotate/Orbit: Alt + Left Click & Drag, or Alt + Left/Right Arrow.

Pan/Move Camera: Alt + Shift + Left Click & Drag, or Alt + Ctrl + Shift + Arrow Keys.

Tilt Camera: Alt + Ctrl + Up/Down Arrow.

Reset Camera: Esc (may require two presses).

Mouselook: Press M to toggle, then move the mouse to look. 

Tips for Better Control
-----------------------

Camera Focus: Hold Alt and left-click an object or your avatar to set it as the new focal point.

Disable Constraints: To move the camera freely, go to the top menu, Advanced (Ctrl-Alt-D) > Disable Camera Constraints.

Camera Smoothing: Alt + arrow keys or mouse movement can be used with camera smoothing in viewers like Firestorm for smoother movements.

Advanced Rotation: Use Alt + Ctrl + Left/Right Arrow to orbit.
