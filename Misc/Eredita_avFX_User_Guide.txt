Eredita avFX: Sound That Moves With You

Second Life is vibrant, visually stunning, and full of creativity—but it’s often silent. Now, with avFX your avatar comes to life like never before!

Customize a vast array of movement and vocal sounds to match your unique virtual presence. Whether it’s footsteps echoing across a marble floor, the rustle of fabric, or expressive voice cues, avFX provides a rich sound experience making your Second Life more dynamic and immersive!



┏━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━•••
┃        Getting Started
┗━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━•••

1‣ Wear avFX
    Simply wear your avFX, and it will start working automatically.

2‣ Config
    To adjust your avFX, wear the provided Config HUD.

3‣ Choose Profile
    For first-time use, be sure to choose your preferred Profile in the Settings Menu.



┏━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━•••
┃        Settings Menu
┗━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━•••

Wear the included Config HUD and click it to open the Main Menu, then select Settings.

➤ Profile
    Choose which Profile Notecard to use. Notecards act as playlists for sound effects.

➤ Volume
    Adjust the master volume percentage to control how loud sounds play.

➤ Toggle Move
    Turn movement sound effects On/Off.

➤ Toggle Vocal
    Turn vocal sound effects On/Off.

➤ Toggle Idle
    Set how frequently idle sound effects play.

➤ Idle Playrate
    Cycles through available rates at which idle sound effects will play.

➤ Refresh Rate
    Choose how often avFX checks your avatar’s state. Lower settings improve responsiveness but may impact sim performance. Default: 0.5 sec (recommended).



┏━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━•••
┃        Customization
┗━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━•••

Adding your own sound effects to avFX is simple!

1‣ Rez & Edit
    Drag avFX onto the ground, right-click, and select Edit.

2‣ Add Sounds
    In the Contents tab, drag in your sound files.

3‣ Copy a Profile
    Grab an existing Profile Notecard from Contents and move it to your inventory.

4‣ Rename & Edit
    Rename the copied Notecard, open it, and update sound file names to match your own. (Use | to separate multiple files.)

5‣ Save & Upload
    Drag the updated Notecard back into Contents.

6‣ Fine-Tune
    Open the Notecard directly from avFX Contents for future edits. Just remember to hit Save!

7‣ Ready to Use
    Your custom Profile will now appear in the HUD Settings menu.

Enjoy a truly personalized sound experience!
