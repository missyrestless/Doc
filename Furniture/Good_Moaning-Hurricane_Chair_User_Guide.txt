***   6 - RESYNCHRONIZATION FEATURE
*************************************************************************

This chair comes with a Resynchronization feature: each time you select a pose, the animations of this pose restart three seconds later. This is not a bug, it is a feature, to ensure that both avatars play their anim in perfect synchronization + the different anims (body, facial, physics) are perfectly synchronized.

That said, if you don't like this, you can disable it. Click the Bed and select Edit to open the Edit Window. Go to the Content Tab. Wait a bit while the content is loading. Then remove the RESYNC notecard. This will disable the Resynchronization feature.
