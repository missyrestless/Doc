ℹ️ How to use and tint the layers: 

▸  The layers are white by default and are pre-tinted in 4 shades (01–04, from light to dark) to make any additional tinting easier if needed. Use the included color guide Hud to find a shade that is closest to the skintone on your fingertips and then right-click "add" the layer. If the layer works right away, you're all set and can skip the tinting part. If further tinting is needed, follow the next steps:

▸  Right-click and "edit" the worn layer in your inventory, click the color/tint window (it should show beige/brown, since it is already pre-tinted) and use the color picker to find your perfect color:

--> https://i.gyazo.com/94189321a0b98e0d88d9a4889e68bf35.gif

--> https://i.gyazo.com/32f02680334805698523f344eddaa580.gif

▸  Once you're happy with your tint, click "OK" in the color picker window and then "<Save" (next to Editing Tattoo) and again "<" (next to Edit Outfit Parts) to leave edit mode

▸  If you want to tint other layers in that same color as well, save your tint to the swatches, by dragging the current color to the swatches at the bottom. That will make it a lot easier for you to get them all in the same color:

--> https://i.gyazo.com/28ced2a64201d320af726418ebc364af.gif
