3) sit on the boat and drive
     a) change the gears with page up/down
     b) change the setting for the gears by pressing the "gears" button 

4) click on the horizontal pole to get the menue for the animations

5) optional/watersport:
     a) wakeboard: click "giv inv." take the board and the grip, wear it -> select the anims waterski 1-3
     
     b) glider/life buoy: 
        
        ~if the owner drives the boat: 
          click "glide" or "ring" - ava 2 has to get up and sit on the glider/life buoy
          when ava 2 sits on the item - click on the boat and select "GliderINIT"
          
        ~if the owner wants to glide:
          click the boat - select "public"
          both avas have to get up
          ava 2 (not the owner) has to sit on the driver position (sit at first)
          owner sits then
          click "glide" or "ring" - and sit on the item (the owner)
          the owner has to click "GliderINIT" then to follow the boat

         "owner" when you leave the boat
