#) LIGHT SETTING
If you click on the rock on the left side, a menu will pop up - you can change
the light-settings there. The main-menu of this site is filled with the colours
you can select.The button "get text" is nothing specific.
If you click on "advanced", another page will pop up where you can
adjust/set the falloff of the light, the intensity and the radius or reset.



#) TEXTURE SETTING
The textures for the waterfall or the plants can be changed in the main menu, if you are the owner.
