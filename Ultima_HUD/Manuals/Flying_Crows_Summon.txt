[SET UP]

IMPORTANT : Be sure to be in a rez and script allowed area and wear the latest version of Ultima HUD

Rez the object "*Ultima HUD* Add-On - Flying Crows" included in this package and TOUCH IT to transfer its contents to your Ultima HUD.
Once the new features added to your HUD, touch again the device to delete it. Keep the copy of your device in you inventory.

Now you can access to the new features added by clicking the HUD + button (just under its logo), this add-on will be listed in "Spells" chapter.
__________________________________________________________
[FEATURES DETAILS & USAGE]

▶ Cast the spell ◀ 
Click the + button of your Ultima HUD and select the "Spells" chapter, then click on "Crows" to summon hundred of flying crows that will follow you around.

▶ Options ◀ 
As soon as the crows appear, a dialog box will pop up to ask you how the crows should act.
By default the crows are following you, but you can also wear them by selecting "Attach" or transform them into a static decorative object by selecting "Static" in the menu.

▶Stop ◀ 
Simply click the "STOP" button of your HUD. Also, if you leave the region, the crows will die by themselves.

*Technical Note*
Low lag & low scripted, no particles used, and only 1 script of 11Kb memory, even if there's lot of smoothly animated crows around you, this spell is low prim count (8 prims land impact) using textures animation.
