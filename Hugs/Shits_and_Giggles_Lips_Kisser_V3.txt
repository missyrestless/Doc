🍥 SHITS & GIGGLES Lips Kisser – User Guide 🍥

Thank you for choosing this irresistibly kissable attachment! 💋 Below you’ll find instructions to customize your settings, manage who gets to smooch you, and make the most of your new flirty gadget.

▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

💋 QUICK START

Attach the kisser to your avatar. This item is unrigged, so some manual adjustment may be necessary to get the perfect fit & position.
Right-click → Edit the worn item, then move/resize until it sits just right.

There are 4 easy ways to open your Kiss Control Panel:
• LONG PRESS: Touch and hold the kisser for 1 second.
• CHAT COMMAND: Type /69 kiss in local chat.
• GESTURE: Activate the included gesture and press SHIFT + K to open the menu anytime.
• UNIVERSAL HUD: Wear the included S&G Universal HUD and click it to easily access all your S&G clickers in one place!

▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

🛠 MAIN MENU OVERVIEW

When you open the menu, you will see three main options:
🔒 ACCESS – Control who gets to kiss you!
🔧 CUSTOMIZE – Adjust your sounds, particles, chat, and more.
❌ CLOSE – Closes the menu.

Inside the 🔧 CUSTOMIZE menu, you will find:
🔊 SOUND – Mute, set volume, or select which sounds to play
💖 PARTICLES – Choose your sparkle style, emojis, or confetti!
💬 CHAT – Toggle chat messages and set anti-spam cooldowns
📏 RANGE – Customize how far away avatars can be to kiss you
▶️ ANIMATION – Toggle the kiss animation on or off
🖥️ SYSTEM – View current settings, check memory, or reset the device

▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

💬 CHAT SETTINGS

• CHAT: Turn text responses ON or OFF (Silent).
• PRIVATE: When ON, chat messages are shown only to you and the toucher. When OFF, they are seen by everyone nearby in local chat.
• ANTI-SPAM: Set a cooldown timer to prevent the same avatar from spamming the chat.

💌 Want to write your own flirty kiss messages?
Edit the included notecard named "Chat Messages" inside the object’s inventory.
Each line = one message.

You can use [A2] in your messages to insert your own name!

Example:
gave [A2] a surprise smooch 💋
couldn’t resist kissing [A2] right on the lips 😘

▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

📏 RANGE SETTINGS

Set the maximum distance another avatar can be to kiss you.
Choose between preset ranges (3m, 5m, 10m, 20m, or No Limit) to control how close they have to get for a kiss.

▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

🔊 SOUND OPTIONS

Make it soft... or make it extra. 💞
• SOUND: Toggle the kissing sound on or off completely.
• VOLUME 🔈: Open the volume submenu to set your kiss volume between 25% and 100%.

🎵 SELECT SOUND (Sound Appliers)
You can install new sounds using the Shits & Giggles Sound Appliers! 
1. Rez your purchased Sound Applier on the ground.
2. Click it to seamlessly install the sound into your worn Kisser.
3. Open your SOUND > SELECT SOUND menu to view it in your list of sounds. It will be activated automatically.

Any sound button with a ▣ next to it is ACTIVE. If you activate multiple sounds, your kisser will cycle through them with every kiss! (Click an active sound again to deactivate it).

▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

💖 PARTICLE OPTIONS

Add sparkle to your kisses with a variety of emoji-based particle effects!

• PARTICLES: Turn visual effects on or off.
• TEXTURE: Choose from preset emojis, or select "Custom" to use your own texture UUID.
• SHUFFLE: Randomize the particle texture with each kiss!

🎉 NEW: CONFETTI MODE!
Toggle between a single floating emoji, or an explosive Confetti burst! When Confetti is turned ON, you will see new buttons to control the intensity:
• LVL 1 – A subtle sprinkle of 10 particles
• LVL 2 – A fun burst of 20 particles
• LVL 3 – A dramatic explosion of 35 particles!

▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

▶️ ANIMATION SETTINGS

Toggle the smooth, flirty animation that plays when your kisser is activated! The animation included is quite subtle - feel free to replace it in the kisser contents with any animation you'd like!

💡 Custom Animations - The script now plays the first animation found in inventory. You can replace the included one with your own (shorter animations < 4 sec work best).

▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

🔒 ACCESS CONTROL

📋 LISTS – Manage who’s allowed (or not allowed) to kiss you:
  LIST – View current list in local chat with clickable profile links
  ADD/REMOVE – Enter a user's UUID to modify the list
  CLEAR – Removes all names from the list

To get an avatar’s UUID, open their profile and copy it from the top.

🌐 PUBLIC – Anyone can kiss you
▫ WHITELIST – Only listed avatars can kiss you
⬛ BLACKLIST – Everyone except listed avatars can kiss you

▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

🖥️ SYSTEM

Opening the System menu will automatically list your current configuration in local chat.

• MEMORY: Checks the current memory usage of the scripts to ensure they are running healthy and efficiently.
• RESET: Restores all display and feature settings to their factory defaults. (This will NOT clear your Whitelists or Blacklists).
• HARD RESET: ☢️ Wipes the entire database. This restores all factory defaults AND completely uninstalls any sound appliers and erases your Whitelists and Blacklists. Use with caution!
