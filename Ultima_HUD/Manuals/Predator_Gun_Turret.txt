[SET UP]

IMPORTANT : Be sure to be in a rez and script allowed area and wear the latest version of Ultima HUD

Rez the object "*Ultima HUD* Add-On M-268 Predator Turret" included in this package and TOUCH IT to transfer its contents to your Ultima HUD.
Once the new features added to your HUD, touch again the device to delete it. Keep the copy of your device in you inventory.

Now you can access to the new features added by clicking the HUD + button (just under its logo), this add-on will be listed in "Attacks" chapter.
__________________________________________________________
[FEATURES DETAILS & USAGE]

▶ Rez the turret ◀ 
Click the + button of your Ultima HUD and select the "Attacks" chapter, then click on "M-268 Turret" to rez a new gun turret. Once rezzed the menu will pop-up.

▶ Menu buttons ◀ 

★ Range ★
Click and select the detection / shoot range of the M-268 Predator Gun Turret, in meters, from 20m. to 300m.

★ Rate ★
Select the fire rate, in seconds, from 0.1s. to 2.0s.

★ Select Target ★
Click to manually select a target (avatar or object).
By default, once activated, the M-268 Predator Gun Turret will fire at the closest avatar, but using the "Select Target" button you can make it shoot at a desired target.

★ White List ★
Click to access the white list sub-menu, an avatar added into the white list won't be detected as a target by the M-268 Predator Gun Turret in its automatic mode.

☆ + ADD + ☆
Click to access the text-box and enter the username of the avatar you wish to add to the white list, please note that it is case sensitive.

☆ - REMOVE - ☆
Click and select a name in the next dialog box to remove it from the white list.

☆ << MENU ☆
Click to go back to the main menu.

★ >ON< / >OFF< ★
☆ >ON< ☆
Click to activate the M-268 Predator Gun Turret. Once activated it will scan for avatars in range and shoot at them automatically.
☆ >OFF< ☆
Click to de-activate the turret / stop shooting at a selected target.

★ !DELETE! ★
Click to delete the M-268 Predator Gun Turret.

★ > EXIT < ★
Click to close the dialog box and exit the menu.

▶ Delete the turret ◀ 
To delete the gun turret, click the "!DELETE!" button in its menu.

[TIPS]

▶ Animated control screen ◀ 
You can easily know if your M-268 Predator is active by looking at its animated control screen, which will power up while it is active.

▶ Move the turret ◀ 
The M-268 Predator Gun Turret is made of a single linked mesh piece, which mean that you can move it at a desired position by editing it (right-click >> edit) the easiest way possible.

[UPDATES]

Free and automatic updates !
Once a new version of the M-268 Predator Gun Turret is released, you will receive it automatically.

To replace a previous version of an add-on in your Ultima HUD, you must first delete the older one from your HUD. To do that, click the "+" icon on your HUD to access the add-ons menu, then click "*DELETE*" in the dialog box, and select the add-on you wish to erase.
You can now install the updated add-on following the set-up instructions provided in this notecard.
