The following are a few tips and common questions:
------------------------------------------------------------------

【?】Where are the poseballs? How do I turn this thing on?
In a ReACT system, there are NO poseballs! To interact with the ReACT compatible product/furniture, simply sit on it!
You can even choose specific pose spots by hovering your mouse around the furniture and look for your mouse arrow to change 
to a SIT icon.

【?】How do I choose Male or Female?
Your role is designated from where you sit. 
The product chooses Male or Female automatically.  You can always SWAP to change to another role.

【?】How do I change the color/texture of the furniture?
Click the furniture and choose Customize to open up all the customizing features it has to offer.
TIP: Aurora is the glow/shadow underneath the furniture
