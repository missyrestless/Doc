GETTING STARTED

Attach the Kisser to your avatar. It's unrigged, so it may need a little manual adjustment for the perfect fit. Right-click → Edit the worn item, then move/resize until it sits just right.

There are 4 ways to open your Kiss Control Panel:

• LONG PRESS – Touch and hold the Kisser for 1 second.
• CHAT COMMAND – Type /69 kiss in local chat.
• GESTURE – Activate the included gesture and press SHIFT + K anytime.
• UNIVERSAL HUD – Wear the included S&G Universal HUD and click it to reach all your S&G clickers in one place.

MAIN MENU

Opening the menu gives you:

🔒 Access – Control who gets to kiss you.
🔧 Customize – Adjust sounds, particles, chat, and more.
💞 Partner – Pair up with someone special and share a heart chain (see below).
🌐 Web Login – Sign in to your online dashboard to manage everything from your browser (see below).
✖️ Close – Closes the menu.

Inside Customize you'll find:

🔊 Sound – Mute, set volume, or pick which sounds play.
💖 Particles – Choose your sparkle style, emojis, or confetti.
💬 Chat – Toggle chat messages and set anti-spam cooldowns.
📏 Range – Set how far away avatars can be to kiss you.
▶️ Animation – Toggle the kiss animation on or off.
🖥️ System – View settings, check memory, or reset the device.

CLOUD SAVE & WEB LOGIN  (NEW in v4.0!)

Your Kisser now saves your settings, installed sounds, Whitelists, and Blacklists safely to our server. You'll never lose your setup again - a fresh copy, or any future update, restores everything for you automatically.

You can also manage your Kisser from any web browser. Open the menu and tap 🌐 Web Login and follow the URL, or attach the Web Dashboard HUD.

From your dashboard you can view and change all your settings and view your stats/leaderboard.

PARTNERS  (NEW in v4.0!)

Pair up with up to 3 special someones and share a lips-to-lips heart chain that flows between you whenever you're close or share a kiss. 💕

Open the menu and tap 💞 PARTNER to manage your pairings:

• Pair – Start a pairing with someone nearby. They'll get a request to accept on their own Kisser - you both need to be in the same region, each wearing your updated Kisser.
• Unpair – Unlink from a partner.
• Particles – Choose the particle texture your chain uses.
• Preview – See your chain effect in action.
• Chain – Turn the heart-chain effect on or off.

CHAT SETTINGS

• Chat – Turn text responses ON or OFF (Silent).
• Private – When ON, messages show only to you and the toucher. When OFF, everyone nearby sees them in local chat.
• Anti-spam – Set a cooldown so the same avatar can't spam the chat.

WRITING YOUR OWN KISS MESSAGES (STEP-BY-STEP)

Want your own flirty lines? The Kisser loads its messages from a notecard in its contents.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ STEP 1: Open the Notecard

Right-click the Kisser (worn or rezzed) and select "Edit". Go to the "Content" tab and double-click the notecard named "Chat Messages".

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ STEP 2: Add Your Lines

Type one message per line. Use [A2] to drop in your own name.

Example:
gave [A2] a surprise smooch 💋
couldn't resist kissing [A2] right on the lips 😘

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ STEP 3: Save

Save the notecard and your new lines are live.

RANGE

Set the maximum distance another avatar can be to kiss you. Choose between 3m, 5m, 10m, 20m, or No Limit.

SOUND

• Sound – Toggle the kissing sound on or off.
• Volume – Set your kiss volume between 25% and 100%.

SELECT SOUND (Sound Appliers)
Install new sounds with the Shits & Giggles Sound Appliers:

1. Rez your purchased Sound Applier on the ground.
2. Click it to install the sound into your worn Kisser.
3. Open SOUND > SELECT SOUND to see it in your list — it activates automatically.

Any sound marked ▣ is ACTIVE. Activate several and the Kisser cycles through them with every kiss. Click an active sound again to deactivate it.

💡 Want to hear a sound before activating it? Preview your whole library from your web dashboard — just tap the ▶ next to any sound. (See Cloud Save & Web Login above.)

MANAGING & UNINSTALLING SOUNDS
• Del Sound – Want to clean up your list? Click this button, then select a custom sound to permanently remove it from your Kisser. (You will get a confirmation prompt first so you don't delete anything by accident!)
• If you are a collector and install more than 8 custom sounds, your menu will automatically generate "Next" and "Prev" pages so you can browse your entire collection seamlessly.

PARTICLES

Add sparkle to every kiss with emoji-based effects.

• Particles – Turn visual effects on or off.
• Texture – Pick a preset emoji, or choose "Custom" for your own texture UUID.
• Shuffle – Randomize the particle texture with each kiss.

CONFETTI MODE
Switch between a single floating emoji and an explosive confetti burst. With Confetti ON, set the intensity:

• LVL 1 – A subtle sprinkle of 10 particles.
• LVL 2 – A fun burst of 20 particles.
• LVL 3 – A dramatic explosion of 35 particles.

ANIMATION

Toggle the smooth, flirty animation that plays when the Kisser fires. The included animation is subtle — swap it for any animation you like by dropping yours into the Kisser's contents.

The script plays the first animation it finds in inventory, so replace the included one with your own (shorter animations under 4 seconds work best).

ACCESS CONTROL

LISTS – Manage who's allowed (or not) to kiss you:

• List – View the current list in local chat with clickable profile links.
• Add / Remove – Enter a user's UUID to modify the list.
• Clear – Removes all names from the list.

To get an avatar's UUID, open their profile and copy it from the top.

• Public – Anyone can kiss you.
• Whitelist – Only listed avatars can kiss you.
• Blacklist – Everyone except listed avatars can kiss you.

Your lists are saved to the cloud too, so they carry over to future updates.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SYSTEM

Opening System lists your current configuration in local chat.

• Memory – Checks current script memory usage to confirm everything's running healthy.
• Reset – Restores display and feature settings to factory defaults. (Does NOT clear your Whitelists or Blacklists.)
• Hard Reset – Wipes the entire database: restores factory defaults AND uninstalls any sound appliers and erases your Whitelists and Blacklists. Use with caution.
