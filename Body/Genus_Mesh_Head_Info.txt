GENUS MESH HEAD INFORMATION

----------------------------------------------------

All packs include:

- Bento Mesh Head

- Default Shape
- Eyebrow Shape
- Eyes (rigged/unrigged)
- Teeth/Tongue

- Alpha Head
- Alpha Eyes 
- Alpha Body
- Alpha Head+Body
- Alpha Eyes+Body
- Redelivery HUD
- Animations Gestures

--------------------------------
HOW TO WEAR IT:
--------------------------------

1. Wear your Genus Head, eyes, teeth/tongue, default shape and the alpha layer.

2. Attach the Genus Universal HUD and Genus Animations HUD.

3. Pay attention, for the correct head view you should wear default shape. After it you can modify Shape.

4. Bakes on Mesh is a feature to allow system avatar baked textures to be shown on mesh attachments. Please make sure your viewer supports BOM system.

5. To activate the BOM for your Genus head you must choose which option you want to use - 4K or Regular head. If you are using 4K bom, then you need to press the 4K button and then select the BOM button of the universal hood. To switch to a regular bom, you need to turn off the 4k bom, turn off the 4k head and then simply press the bom button.

6. If you see a red head after activating the BOM or transparent pieces of the head, then for correctly display the textures, you need to remove the alpha layer from the avatar's head.
After that, you can use the system textures for your head.

 7. If you use a ordinary skin applier, the head will no longer be BOM activated. To activate BOM you must repeat point 5.

8. Body Skin: You need to purchase VELOUR Body Skins at Velour mainstore to match the Genus 4K skin
