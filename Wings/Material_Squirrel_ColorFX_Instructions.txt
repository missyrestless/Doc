Installation:

-  Rez your wings on the ground
-  Right-Click on your wings and choose 'Edit'
-  Click the 'More' button to display the tabs (if edit window is half closed)
-  Choose the 'Content' tab
-  Delete the script named 'Xcite! interface 4.2 - 1/21'
-  Wait a few seconds for the Content tab to refresh, and verify that the script has been deleted
-  Drag and drop the two scripts that came with this file into the contents. The scripts are:
    1)  Material Squirrel ColourFX v1.00
       and
    2)  Xcite! interface 4.2.2 - 4/9
- Take the wings back into your inventory
- Wear the wings
- (optional) Click the 'Reset' button on your Material Squirrel HUD

Thats it! The scripts can be copied, so you can place them in all your Material Squirrel wings

Usage:

From Menus
Click on your wings to bring up the blue dialog menu. 
Then click on 'ColorFX'.
The ColorFX Dialog will start. If you do not get a blue dialog menu, turn on 'Touch Xcite' using your Material Squirrel HUD.
 
From Chat
    Use channel 959 to communicate with you Material Squirrel Wings.
    /959 ColorFX starts the ColorFX Dialog
    /959 <any dialog option> will start the effect with that name. Eg "/959 dead" or "/959 rainbow" or "/959 flows"


Color Effects:
 - HeartBeat  : The wings pulse (brighten) at a normal pulse rate. It takes a couple of seconds to 'beat' regularly.
 - FastHeart   : as above but for an "excited" heart rate
 - Angry           : The wings change gradually to a dark red from their current color
 - Scared        : The wings change gradually (but quickly) to a dark grey
 - Dead           : The wings change gradually to black over 60 seconds
 - Recover      : The wings change gradually back to their default color
 - Default      : Turns off all color effect. The wings also go back to all defaults.
 - Flow            : Opens a dialog with a set of color pairs or "Rainbow". The wings cycle slowly through the colors 
                        selected, or though all the colors of the rainbow

Note: Since the 'ColorFX' button is on the main menu, this means you can only have 9 custom Xcite commands in the 'self' category.
